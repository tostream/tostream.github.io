Restart my Leetcode journey:

It has been over 2 years of my last leetcode submission.I wasn't aware I will come back.

I would always suggest to have a book instead of randomly find some resources from Internet.
At least, the sequence of the topic has been organized. It comes with a purpose, maybe the level of difficulty.
