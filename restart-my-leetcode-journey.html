Restart my Leetcode journey:

It has been over 2 years of my last leetcode submission.I wasn't aware I will come back. After going back to be an engineer, I found how weak I am and how useful it is of the algorithms and Data Structures. Going back to Leetcode is difficult. It looks like you know everything, but you just cannot remember when you should brute force to solve it asap.(I mean from a context perspective)

I would always suggest to have a book instead of randomly find some resources from Internet. At least, the sequence of the topic has been organized. It comes with a purpose, maybe the level of difficulty.
